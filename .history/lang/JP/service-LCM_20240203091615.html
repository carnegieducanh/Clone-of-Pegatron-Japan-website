LCM service

Centralized management of the PCs lifecycle from procurement to disposal is an urgent issue for companies, organizations, and municipalities to reduce TCO (*1), and its demand is expected to grow at an accelerated pace in the future.

(*1) Total cost of ownership from procurement to disposal.

Pegatron Japan provides a comprehensive service menu that covers the entire PC lifecycle from procurement to disposal to back up your needs.
