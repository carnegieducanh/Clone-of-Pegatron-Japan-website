Our strength

• Quality & Engineering

Pegatron Japan brings to the Japanese market the know-how and the experience that its parent company, Pegatron Corporation, has accumulated through its business with global customers , in collaboration with the manufacturing and repair sites that the parent company has established all over the world.

• Shopfloor system

Pegatron Japan's shopfloor operating system is based on Pegatron's global platform and it can be partially customized to meet our Japanese customers' needs.

• Speed to revolutionize customer's biz

For example, we linked a customer's business system and their logistics partner's business system with our business system via EDI and established a system for real-time mutual sharing of information in less than six months. This sense of speed is an advantage that our Japanese competitors do not have.

• Diversity

The diverse experience, values, and know-how of our Japanese, Chinese, Taiwanese, and Vietnamese and other international employees enables us to deliver high quality repair services and superior IT solutions from varying perspectives that deliver success and satisfaction for our customers.
