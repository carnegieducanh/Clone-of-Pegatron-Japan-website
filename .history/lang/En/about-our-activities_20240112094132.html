Our Activities

At PEGATRON, we believe that not only can technology significantly impact product quality, but people also have a significant influence as well.

Therefore, we have invested heavily in a learning system for our employees. Through multi-dimensional learning sessions, along with continuous improvement and innovation, we strive to enhance the added-value of our products and improve production process capabilities.

Our ultimate goal is to provide high quality products and reliable services to our customers. All of our manufacturing sites have passed ISO 9001 Quality Management System certification.

Pegatron has implemented clean production processes, a green supply chain, and green production to demonstrate its commitment and responsibility to the environment.

Our commitment to the environment is highlighted by Green Partner certification, ISO 14001 Environmental Management System certification, and IECQ QC 080000 Hazardous Substance Process Management System certification for all our global manufacturing sites.

Also, we have a comprehensive occupational health and safety management system and have passed OSHAS 18001 Occupational Health and Safety Management certification. We strive to reduce occupational safety and health risks by risk assessment, training and health promotion activities that in turn raise the health and safety awareness of employees.

In light of the growing climate change issues, we have begun to explore the greenhouse gas inventory of the entire organization and have been awarded ISO 14064 certification.
