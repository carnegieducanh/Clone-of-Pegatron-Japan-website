Corporate Culture

Diversity, Work-life balance, and Encouraging innovation are the keys to our continuous development.

Diversity

We are committed to respecting diversity to foster collaborations and value integration. We believe that our culture will encourages employees to reach their full potential leading to new ideas and innovation.

• We strive to build multinational collaboration and an environment of open information sharing. Our employees come from ten different countries mainly from Japan, China, Taiwan, and Vietnam.
• As one of our management strategies is to promote sustainable growth, the active participation of female employees is critical. As a results, we encourage female proactive involvement and female leadership.
• To create a multi-skilled workforce, we endeavor to provide high-level education to facilitate individual know-how development. In that way, our employees are able to advance their careers leading to sustainable company development.

Work-Life Balance

We believe that "a fulfilling life has a positive impact on our work. A fuller life improves work efficiency, which leads to increased customer value and satisfaction.

We value work-life balance and will create an environment that allows employees to spend more time with their families, improve their physical and mental health, and take time for themselves. At Pegatron Japan:
• The mindset of, "I want to contribute to the company while developing and growing myself" is supported.
• We've established an environment that allows housewives, moms taking care of children, and students to work with flexibility. In 2022, our acquisition rate for childcare and maternity leave was 100%.
• More than 120 days of vacation each year, annual leave taking rate is higher than average at 76.9% (data as of 2022). Additionally, we are attempting to implement flexible scheduling.


Team-work • Collaboration

We promote a culture of collaboration, teamwork, information sharing, and other forms of mutual help and cooperation.

Through team activities, we help each other learn new skills, grow together, and share our individual and organizational growth to create synergy between individuals and the organization. We collaborate not only within the organization, but also with customers and external partners to jointly create advanced products and services.

Encouraging innovation

We encourage innovations by supporting everyone to take on new challenges everyday. One of our key projects is the innovation theme of, "Everyone can do it in order to continuously innovate working methods, everytime - everywhere."

The variety of our employees backgrounds, perspectives and experiences help us to provide innovative customer service.
