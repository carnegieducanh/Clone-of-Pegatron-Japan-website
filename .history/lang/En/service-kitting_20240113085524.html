Kitting

Kitting involves the initial configuration conducted prior to the launch of a computer or smartphone. Typically, during device deployment, tasks include installing the operating system, software, and configuring the connection to the company network. Additionally, kitting work is also undertaken during OS upgrades or equipment replacements.

In summary, the Kitting service encompasses the entire process following the purchase of devices, from unboxing to the initial setup, preparing them for customer use

Advantages of Kitting Service

• Alleviate the burden on internal IT teams

• Streamline initial setup and installation

• Mitigate the risk of setup errors, supported by experienced professionals

• Early fault detection: promptly identify and address any faulty products

• Eliminate the need for additional workspace or storage
