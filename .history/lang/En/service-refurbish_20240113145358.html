Refurbish

We can repair and recondition PCs to the almost same level as when shipped new.

We can provide quick turnaround for PCs that were initially defective from stores, returned PCs, PCs damaged in their boxes during transporation, and PCs that require sudden rework.

We can also provide resale services for refurbished PCs.

In addition, in order to reduce the number of discarded units as much as possible and reduce the burden on the global environment, we can purchase PCs that are no longer needed due to model changes, etc.

It is also possible for these PCS to reenter the market after data deletion and the refurbishing processhas been completed.
